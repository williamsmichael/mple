About

I am an aspiring software developer and consultant. I am also a husband, a veteran, and a career-changer. I served five years in the United States Air Force pharmacy. During my first tour, my wife and I married while stationed in England. Post-military, I worked as a Financial Advisor.

Since then, we have been fortunate to live as expats in Austria and Sweden. Stateside includes Arizona and now Portland, Oregon. When I am not coding or helping others, I enjoy exercising, traveling, and exploring.

Through code schools, self-study, volunteering, and consulting roles, my goal as a developer is to build strong collaborations with leading experts across various industries and bring simplicity to technically challenging environments with the end-user in mind.

If you would like to follow me on Twitter, my username is @michael_mdw.
