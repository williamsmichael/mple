Miss South Asia Europe Contest


Registration


Here comes the opportunity for you to become the Miss South Asia Europe 2014 pageant!


Contestants Requirements:

  • - Any young girl between the age of 17-27
  • - Must be a South Asian currently a resident of Europe (Regardless of the place of birth)
  • - The applicant is not/has never been married never given birth to a child is not currently pregnant or a parent
  • - IT'S THE AUTHENTIC 'YOU' THAT WILL MAKE A DIFFERENCE

Miss South Asia Europe Pageant Segments:

  • - Indian Traditional segment for all
  • - Evening Gown segment for all
  • - Talent Round segment for top 10
  • - Question & Answer segment for top 5
  • - NO SWIMSUIT ROUND

PROFESSIONAL TRAINING AND GROOMING SESSIONS WILL BE PROVIDED FOR ALL CONTESTANTS SELECTED AS FINALISTS AFTER THE AUDITIONS.
